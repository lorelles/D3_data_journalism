Have Correlations Been Discovered Between Health Risks and Age/Income?

Utilizing data from the US Census Bureau, this chart displays correlations between different demographics such as income and smoking.

Click on each item on the axes to choose between age or income, and smoking or obesity, respectively. Simply hover over each state's data points to display the numbers associated with the given correlation.

The data provided for this chart was obtained from the 2014 ACS 1-year estimates from the US Census Bureau.
